What is an IQ Test after all?

An IQ Test, formally known to as an “intellectual quotient” test, and comes in many forms. They can help diagnose intellectual disabilities or measure someone's intellectual potential through a series or rigorous questions and scenarios.

Who created the IQ Test?

Alfred Binet

Alfred Binet was a psychologist who created a test that tests a person's intelligence. In the early 1900s. Binet was asked by the French government to help create a test to decide whether or not students were struggling in school and needed specialized assistance.

Théodore Simon

Théodore Simon was also a psychologist and colleague of Alfred Binet. Simon helped Binet in the creation of the first ever IQ test. The goal of the test was to target the young audience of 3 to 12 year old children.
